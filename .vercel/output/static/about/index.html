About Refiners City

Transforming lives through Christ's love

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Refiners City Church began with a small group of believers committed to creating a welcoming community where people could encounter God's love and experience authentic transformation.

Over the years, we've grown into a vibrant congregation dedicated to serving our community and spreading the message of hope and redemption through Jesus Christ.

Our mission remains unchanged: to be a place where people can belong, believe, and become all that God has called them to be.

Our Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and our guide for faith and life.

Salvation

Salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Church

The church is a community of believers called to worship, grow, and serve together.

Mission

We are called to share God's love and make disciples of all nations.
