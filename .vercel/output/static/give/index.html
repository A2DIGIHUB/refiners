Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and a reflection of our gratitude for God's blessings. Your faithful support enables us to:

  • Maintain and improve our facilities
  • Support local and global missions
  • Provide resources for our ministries
  • Help those in need in our community
  • Invest in the next generation

Every gift, regardless of size, makes a difference in advancing God's kingdom through the work of Refiners City Church.
